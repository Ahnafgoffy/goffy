FACTS ABOUT TIGERS

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world, known for their strength, unique stripes, and solitary hunting behavior.

Tigers can weigh up to 363 kg (800 pounds) and measure up to 3.3 meters (10.8 feet) in length

They primarily eat large mammals such as deer, wild pigs, antelope, and buffalo
